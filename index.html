About Me

Hi! My name is Eric Inman. I was born and raised in the blistering heat of Bakersfield, California. Ever since I was little, I had a fascination with computers and always wondered how they worked. That same curiosity led me to pursue a higher education. I graduated from California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo with a bachelor's in Software Engineering and a master's in Computer Science. I'm also a 2023 T35 recipient at the University of South Carolina, Columbia where I will be researching infectious diseases using data science. Professionally, I've been very fortunate to have done an internship with a health tech startup company called PyrAmes Health Inc. where I worked on building a deep-learning model for classifying motion artifacts in blood pressure monitoring data. Academically, I'm very interested in machine learning, deep-learning, and their applications in healthcare. When I'm not at the computer, I really enjoy baseball, reading, and good barbecue.
